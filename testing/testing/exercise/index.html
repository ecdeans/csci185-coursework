Dungeons and Dragons for Beginners

The Basics

At its core, Dungeons and Dragons is a fun interactive storytelling game. It's an opportunity to get together with friends and have a good time slaying dragons, hopping dimensions, or even opening up a lucrative local tavern.

However, it's understandable that D&D could seem daunting to newbies. One look at the 5th edition character sheet and its undeniable. There are a lot of little sections with numbers and stats that look like a foreign language at first glance. But stick with us, because once you get the hang of a few basic ideas everything becomes a lot more readable. And, you might just find the game opening up before you.

Polyhedral Dice

D&D has an element of luck to it. When players attempt to attack an enemy, or take certain actions, a roll of the dice may be asked for!

Actual Play Shows

Actual play shows like Critical Role and Dimension 20 are responsible for introducing many new players to D&D.
